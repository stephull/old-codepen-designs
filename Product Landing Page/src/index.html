About Us

At Edgren Inc. Technologies, we pride ourselves in being the frontier for green innovation. We specialize in using technology and engineering to develop new solutions to help our environment.

The services we provide are created by new and experienced employees and innovators alike. We take pride in being equitable to all engineers and software developers, giving them opportunities to enter the workforce and to begin their path in helping the Earth. We tailor solutions to help employees solve real-life problems and to help get everyone's foot in the right direction.

Our award-winning technologies range from improved wastewater treatment along the West Coast, to solar panel projects in Mexico, both of which have resulted in amazing results in satisfaction, energy renewal, and reduction in pollution.

Goals

Our biggest goal has been implementing a solar farm in Mexico. It is one of the largest solar farms in North America.

Our commitments have led to a 7000% increase in solar power energy across Baja California and Northern Mexico regions of Mexico. We've also increased projects for renewable energy with solar panel projects in Arizona, and a 1000% increase in renewable energy via wind turbine installations in the Eastern U.S.

We were recognized as being one of the Top Ten Best Companies for Renewable Energy according to the Renewable Energy Magazine in 2015, and again in 2017, both coming in only after Cascadia Power.
We are honored that our commitments have impacted the world for the better!
